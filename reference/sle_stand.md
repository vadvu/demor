# Data on Standard Life expectancies that is used for YLL calculations

Data on Standard Life expectancies that is used for YLL calculations

## Usage

``` r
sle_stand
```

## Format

### A dataframe

- stand:

  Standard: 1 - World Health Organization Standard Life Expectancy by
  single-age; 2 - Global Burden of Disease studies (GBD) and WHO Global
  Health Estimates (WHO GHE) Standard Life Expectancy by 5-year age
  groups.

- age:

  age

- ex:

  Standard Life Expectancy

## Source

Martinez, R., Soliz, P., Caixeta, R., Ordunez, P.
[2019](https://doi.org/10.1093/ije/dyy254). Reflection on modern
methods: years of life lost due to premature mortality—a versatile and
comprehensive measure for monitoring non-communicable disease mortality.
International Journal of Epidemiology 48, 1367–1376.
